  • Mission

    The C.O.D.E. Initiative is a not-for-profit organization with a mission to help kids on the autism spectrum find passion in learning by focusing on teaching them to explore the skill of coding and computer programming.

    Our main focus is to help children with Autism Spectrum Disorder(ASD) find a passion and interest in learning through exposing them to coding. We hope to touch their lives in a meaningful way by introducing them to a skill that can potentially improve their livelihood and help them reach independence. Too often, children with ASD struggle to succeed under the traditional school system because most institutions do not have that one-to-one teacher to student ratio that improves the learning environment for these kids.
